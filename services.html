West77 Partners is a vertically integrated real estate investment and development firm. We control every stage of the real estate lifecycle — from acquisition and entitlement through construction, operations, asset management, and disposition. This integration allows us to capture value at every phase, reduce risk, and deliver superior outcomes for our investors.

The services West77 Partners provides are driven by our desire to prudently invest, not the other way around. Our senior executives have been developing properties for over 20 years across multiple market cycles. We strongly believe there is a time to develop, a time to acquire, and a time to hold — and that discipline defines everything we do.

Acquisition

West77 Partners pursues geographical diversification in growth markets where we have knowledge and experience to offset economic risk from single market concentration. We typically seek to build critical mass in a locale in order to yield management economies of scale and avoid single property isolation.

We have extensive relationships with banking institutions, attorneys, title companies, and others in the real estate industry with whom we regularly work to identify properties for potential acquisition.

Geographic Diversification

Strategic positioning across high-growth U.S. markets to offset concentration risk.

02

Critical Mass Strategy

Building density in key locales for management economies of scale.

03

Industry Relationships

Extensive banking, legal, and brokerage networks to source off-market opportunities.

Selectively acquiring non-performing commercial real estate notes from financial institutions.

Asset Management

Increasing asset value for our investors

West77 Partners approaches asset management from the ownership perspective. We apply a comprehensive, proactive approach to the commercial properties we oversee to maximize net operating income for our investors. As stewards of our assets, we are active participants in debt strategy & covenant tracking, operational expense management, physical property audits and regular portfolio reviews with our onsite property managers.

We pride ourselves on being operators instead of mere passive investors in real estate. By actively engaging in the management of the investment on a daily basis, we provide a real time response to critical issues affecting both the health of the asset and its financial results. Our overarching goal is to enhance the investment’s value by increasing revenue, minimizing expenses, and maximizing perceived asset value.

01

Debt Strategy & Covenant Tracking

Active monitoring and management of debt obligations, covenants, and refinancing opportunities to optimize the capital structure of each asset.

02

Operational Expense Management

Disciplined cost control across every property, identifying efficiencies that protect margins and enhance net operating income.

03

Physical Property Audits

Regular on-site inspections and brand-standard reviews to maintain property condition, guest satisfaction, and long-term asset value.

04

Portfolio Performance Reviews

Ongoing collaboration with onsite property managers through regular portfolio reviews, revenue optimization, and strategic planning.
