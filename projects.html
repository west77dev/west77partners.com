Case Studies

We highlight three projects that faced unique challenges to illustrate West77 Partners’ commitment to successful outcomes for our contracting partners, lenders and investors. Each case study is a testament to the culture of West77 Partners, the commitment to hands-on leadership of our executive team and an appreciation for our responsibility to be ardent stewards of our investor’s capital.

Project Photo

Washington Square Condos

Bellevue, WA

An urban mixed-use development comprising 353 high-rise condominiums across two towers, 26 urban townhomes, 10,000 square feet of retail, and an underground parking garage with over 750 stalls. Mr. Nielson served as Managing Director, overseeing acquisition, entitlements, design, construction, and the sale of all 379 units — including navigating the workout and payoff of a $153 Million US Bank led syndicated loan facility during the Great Financial Crisis.

Project Photo

LivAway Suites West Jordan

West Jordan, UT

The inaugural LivAway Suites property and proof of concept for the brand’s affordable extended stay model. Completed on time and under budget, this property demonstrated the viability of ~13 month build times and rapid stabilization, achieving 80%+ occupancy within 90 days of opening.
