About West77 Partners

Building the Future of
Extended Stay Hospitality

Affordable extended stay lodging remains one of the highest-performing asset classes in commercial real estate, yet it remains under-institutionalized relative to its potential. We believe this space is on the cusp of the same institutional adoption and value expansion that self-storage experienced over the past two decades.

West77 Partners is a vertically integrated real estate investment and development firm with exclusive national franchise rights to LivAway Suites®. Our platform offers investors the opportunity to participate early in that evolution at a scale unparalleled in the marketplace.
